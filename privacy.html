Privacy Policy

Effective: March 17, 2026

Calisthenics Tracker ("we", "the app") is developed by Felix as an independent project. This policy explains how data is handled when you use the app.

1. Data We Collect

The app stores your workout data (exercises, sets, reps, hold times, bodyweight, notes) locally on your device. If you create an account, your email address is collected for authentication purposes only.

2. Data Storage

All training data is stored on-device by default. If cloud sync is enabled in a future update, data will be stored on secured servers and you will be notified before any sync occurs.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

• Apple HealthKit – only if you explicitly grant permission. We read/write workout data; this data is never sent to external servers.
• RevenueCat – for managing in-app purchases. RevenueCat's own privacy policy applies.
• Expo / EAS – for app updates. Anonymous device metadata may be collected for crash reporting.

4. Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics or ad tracking. No personal data is sold or shared with advertisers.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by removing your account in the app settings or by uninstalling the app. To request manual deletion, contact us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

8. Contact
