Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-03-17

Calisthenics Tracker ("we", "the app") is developed by Felix as an independent project. This policy explains how your data is handled.

1. Data We Collect

The app stores your workout data — exercises, sets, reps, hold times, bodyweight, and notes — locally on your device. If you create an account, your email address is collected for authentication purposes only.

2. Data Storage

All training data is stored on-device by default. If cloud sync is enabled in a future update, data will be stored on secured servers and you will be notified before any sync occurs.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following services:

HealthKit Only if you explicitly grant permission. Workout data read/written locally — never sent to external servers.
RevenueCat Manages in-app purchases. Their own privacy policy applies.
Expo / EAS Handles app updates. Anonymous device metadata may be collected for crash reporting.

4. Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics or ad tracking. No personal data is sold or shared with advertisers.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete all data at any time by removing your account in Settings or by uninstalling the app. For manual deletion, contact us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a revised effective date.
